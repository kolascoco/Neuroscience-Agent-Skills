## Why This Matters For The Agent

This abstract directly links DLPFC target selection, muscle artifact, early-local TEP reliability, and analytical parameter choices.

## Main TEP Nature Points

Early-local TEPs can vary by DLPFC target; larger amplitudes do not automatically mean better or cleaner physiological signals.

## Methodological Tips And Nuances

The abstract notes that broader windows such as `20-60 ms` and peak-to-peak quantification may improve reliability, but treat this as abstract-level guidance until full methods are available.

## Agent Rules Extracted
