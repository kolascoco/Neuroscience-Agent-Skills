## Planning Focus

SMA/pre-SMA planning requires precise subregion targeting and careful distinction between excitability and connectivity endpoints.

## Risks

- nearby subregions with different profiles
- E-field uncertainty
- connectivity versus local excitability overclaim

## Practical Advice

Use neuronavigation/E-field modeling when available, report coordinates precisely, and predefine whether the endpoint is local TEP, source response, connectivity, or behavior.
