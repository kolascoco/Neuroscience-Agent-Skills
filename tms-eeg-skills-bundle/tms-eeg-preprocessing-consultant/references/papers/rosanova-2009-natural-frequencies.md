## Why This Matters For The Agent

Classic source for the idea that perturbation with TMS can reveal target-specific natural frequencies.

## Main TEP Nature Points

TEP peaks can be interpreted partly as oscillatory responses of corticothalamic systems, not only isolated deflections.

## Methodological Tips And Nuances

When users discuss TEP peaks or rhythms, ask whether they analyze time-frequency content, target region, and participant-specific rhythms.

## Agent Rules Extracted

- Load for natural-frequency or oscillatory TEP questions.
- Link TEP frequency to target and state, not just component latency.

## Claims To Avoid

Do not infer oscillatory generators from time-domain peaks alone.
