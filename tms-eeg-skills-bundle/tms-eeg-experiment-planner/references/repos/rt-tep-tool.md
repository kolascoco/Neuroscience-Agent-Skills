## Role In The Skill

Use the rt-TEP concept as an online QC and target-engagement workflow: visualize short-average TEPs before formal collection to tune coil orientation, intensity, target, and artifact controls.

## Planning Use

Relevant when deciding whether a target/setup produces an interpretable early local response and manageable artifact profile.

## Failure Modes

Treating short online averages as final analysis; optimizing only amplitude while ignoring muscle/sensory artifacts; failing to retune masking after intensity changes.
