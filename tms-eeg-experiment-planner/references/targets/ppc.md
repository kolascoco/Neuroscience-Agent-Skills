## Planning Focus

Parietal targets may offer lower muscle artifact than frontal sites and can be useful for source-level TEPs or i-TEP exploration.

## Risks

- target precision and source/topography interpretation
- natural-frequency/state effects
- early-window artifact interpretation for i-TEPs

## Practical Advice

Use neuronavigation, define ROI/source analysis beforehand, and monitor early local response online. For i-TEP designs, document sampling, lead configuration, and artifact window.
