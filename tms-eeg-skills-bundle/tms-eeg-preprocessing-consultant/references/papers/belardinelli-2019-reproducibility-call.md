## Why This Matters For The Agent

This short article supports the skill's emphasis on data sharing, standard procedures, and experimental controls for reliable TMS-EEG.

## Main TEP Nature Points

The reproducibility of TEPs depends on disentangling genuine cortical responses from sensory activation and methodological variability.

## Methodological Tips And Nuances

Mention data sharing, standardization, effective controls, and careful sensory matching.
