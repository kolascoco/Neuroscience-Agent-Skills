## Why This Matters For The Agent

Early reproducibility source for TMS-evoked EEG responses.

## Main TEP Nature Points

TEP reproducibility is an empirical property requiring consistent stimulation, acquisition, preprocessing, and analysis.

## Methodological Tips And Nuances

For reproducibility questions, ask about session spacing, neuronavigation, coil orientation, intensity, trial count, and preprocessing consistency.

## Agent Rules Extracted

- Load for test-retest or reliability questions.
- Treat reproducibility as target- and metric-specific.

## Claims To Avoid

Do not assume reproducibility from one component, target, or protocol.
