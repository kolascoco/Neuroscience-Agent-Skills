## Use When

Use when the user asks about PyTepFit, recurrent large-scale network dynamics, local versus recurrent TEP components, or model-based interpretation of cleaned TMS-EEG evoked responses.

## Required Inputs

Already preprocessed TEPs, source/channel analysis target, model configuration, structural/connectome assumptions, lead-field/source information when applicable, and the specific question being modeled.

## Workflow Order

1. Confirm preprocessing and QC are complete.
2. Confirm empirical TEPs are suitable for modeling and not dominated by residual artifacts.
3. Load PyTepFit repo card and Momi et al. paper card.
4. Verify current repository instructions and dependencies.
5. Treat modeling outputs as interpretation aids, not preprocessing outputs.
6. Report assumptions about connectome, neural mass model, lead field, fitted parameters, and empirical data source.

## Step Cards To Load

Load `steps/tepfit-modeling-analysis.md`, TEP averaging/metric cards, QC cards, PyTepFit repo card, and Momi et al. paper card.

## Parameter Defaults To Consider

Do not invent model parameters, data paths, atlas settings, or PyTepFit APIs. Verify in GitHub fallback because Context7 may not have this repo.

## QC Checkpoints

Confirm cleaned empirical TEP quality, model/data alignment, channel/source space consistency, fit metrics, and whether conclusions are robust to assumptions.

## Common Failure Modes

Using PyTepFit as if it preprocesses raw data, fitting artifact-contaminated TEPs, overinterpreting model parameters, and ignoring sensory confounds in empirical TEPs.

## Learning Mode Response

Explain the local-versus-recurrent dynamics question and why modeling complements but does not replace preprocessing QC.

## Code-Engineer Mode Response

Use verified PyTepFit README/notebooks and dependencies. If APIs are unclear, give setup/source-inspection steps rather than runnable code.

## Claims And Caveats

Model fits can support hypotheses about recurrent dynamics, but they do not prove that a cleaned TEP component is artifact-free or uniquely generated by one mechanism.
