## Why This Matters For The Agent

Neuronavigation affects reproducibility, target precision, and interpretation of TEP variability, but it is not itself a preprocessing step.

## Main TEP Nature Points

TEPs depend on where and how stimulation is delivered. Targeting inconsistency can masquerade as physiological variability.

## Methodological Tips And Nuances

Mention neuronavigation when users compare sessions, targets, subjects, or source-level TEPs.

## Agent Rules Extracted

- In preprocessing answers, treat neuronavigation as acquisition metadata.
- Route detailed navigation/E-field questions to a future experiment-planning skill.

## Claims To Avoid

Do not imply preprocessing can fix poor targeting consistency.
