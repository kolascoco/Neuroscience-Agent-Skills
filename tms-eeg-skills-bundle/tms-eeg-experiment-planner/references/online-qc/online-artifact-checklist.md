## Before Main Collection

- Check impedance/contact quality and unstable electrodes.
- Confirm trigger timing and event labels.
- Inspect single trials around TMS pulse.
- Mark pulse artifact duration, recharge/discharge, and decay behavior.
- Inspect craniofacial/scalp muscle activity.
- Check auditory masking and participant comfort.
- Compare active and sham/control artifact profile if possible.
- Verify coil tracking and target stability.

## During Collection

- Recheck artifact profile after intensity/orientation changes.
- Monitor trial rejection risk and channel drift.
- Retune masking if intensity changes.
- Pause if pulse, decay, or muscle artifact changes suddenly.

## Output Rule

Every protocol should include online QC screenshots/logs and a go/no-go rule before full acquisition.
