## Why This Matters For The Agent

Supports the principle that coil orientation matters and should be treated as an optimization variable.

## Planning Rules Extracted

- Do not assume one orientation is always optimal.
- Include orientation in protocol and reporting.
- When feasible, compare candidate orientations with online physiological/artifact readouts.
