## Why This Matters For The Agent

This paper anchors real-time TEP monitoring as a practical acquisition strategy to maximize cortical activation and minimize artifacts.

## Planning Rules Extracted

- Use online visualization before full acquisition.
- Treat early response quality and artifact level as adjustable acquisition variables.
- Use short averages for setup optimization, not final inference.

## Methodological Nuances

rt-TEP helps with target/coil/intensity choices but does not replace sham/sensory controls or offline QC.
